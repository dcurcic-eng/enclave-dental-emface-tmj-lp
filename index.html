QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

TMJ & EMFACE, EXPLAINED

What is TMJ dysfunction and how does it develop?

It occurs when the jaw joint and surrounding muscles are stressed, inflamed, or imbalanced. Common causes include teeth grinding, clenching, bite misalignment, injury, and chronic stress. Symptoms range from jaw pain and clicking to headaches and limited mouth opening.

How does Emface treat TMJ?

Emface combines HIFES muscle stimulation with synchronized radiofrequency. HIFES triggers deep, involuntary contractions that re-educate and relax the masseter and temporalis, while radiofrequency warms the tissue to ease inflammation and support the joint as your muscles rebalance.

Is Emface FDA-cleared for TMJ?

Yes. Emface is FDA-cleared, and at Enclave Dental we use it as part of a dental-led protocol focused on the muscular root of TMJ dysfunction.

How many sessions do I need?

A typical course is four 20-minute sessions, usually scheduled twice a week over two weeks. Your exact plan is confirmed at your consultation.

Does it hurt?

No. Most patients find the sessions relaxing. You feel rhythmic muscle contractions and gentle warmth, with no needles, no incisions and no downtime.

Can TMJ be treated without surgery or injections?

For many patients, yes. Emface is completely non-surgical and injection-free, targeting overactive jaw muscles directly rather than masking symptoms.

How quickly will I feel relief?

Many patients feel their first relief after one or two sessions, with the full effect developing two to four weeks after the final session.
